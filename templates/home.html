About Our Society

The Institution of Engineers (India) [IEI] is a statutory body to promote and advance engineering and technology, established in 1920 and incorporated by Royal Charter in 1935. It is the largest multi-disciplinary professional body of engineers encompassing 15 engineering disciplines with a membership of more than 820 thousand, serving the nation for more than 9 decades. The IEI has its headquarters located in Kolkata with a national presence through more than 100 Centres and several Overseas Chapters, Foras, and Organs. IEI (Institution of Engineers India) Thapar Chapter is a technical society of Thapar University which organizes various workshops, technical exams, and lecture series by industry experts. This society is open for all technical branches of engineering.
